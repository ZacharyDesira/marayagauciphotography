About Me

Hi I'm Maraya!

Thanks for stopping by, so here's a little about me.

I'm a 16 year old currently reading a diploma in professional photography but meanwhile I get to practice this profession part time. Taking on this initiative has given me so much experiene already and I can affirm that this is what I want to do in my life. Photography was not my first choice of career but it more came to me by chance. I have always had an eye for detail and saw beauty in the litle things that most people miss to see. Then one day I got the opportunity to take charge of a family wedding which I prepared for so well that right when I was editing those beautiful perfect captured moments I realised - This is what I want to do!

And that was just the begining of my photography business. I have now invested so much and have gotten so much amazing feedback it encourages me to imporve and strive to get creative with my shoots. Dedication and being organised is my primary focus for each and every service I am being enrusted with and so far my clients were always happily impressed. I love the opportunities this journey has given me, I get to meet new people everyday, new stories, new journeys, and I get to be part of these beautiful moments! I am grateful for each and every one of these life celebrations and make sure to capture those perfect little moments for them. I also love a challenge, experimenting with my skills and my camera's ability to get creative and get an outstanding final result!

Take a look at my portfolio and if you like what you see just fill in the request form to book in your photoshoot below!
